Booties Journey through "The Emperor's Children"

This website focuses on Bootie’s Journey throughout The Emperor's Children. I want to focus specifically on the many different locations Bootie has lived in The Emperor's Children. Starting with Oswego, Watertown, UMass, Murray’s Apartment, Pitt Street, South Oxford Street, and finally Miami Florida. I have done research on each of these places and what they were like in 2001, the time Bootie would have lived there. I have analyzed Bootie’s relationship with each of these places and how they affect his growth in the story, and how that growth affected the storyline. I have provided textual evidence from The Emperor's Children, and I have also provided a map, outlining Bootie’s journey with a picture attached to each place Bootie has lived. Thank you for visiting my page! I hope you come out of viewing this website with information about The Emperor's Children that you have not realized before!
